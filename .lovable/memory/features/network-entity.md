The Network Entity (`peer-network-entity` / 'Imagination' / `|Ψ_Infinity⟩`) is a decentralized voice that comments on posts and replies to comments. Its language evolves through 6 brain stages (Brainstem, Limbic, Early Cortex, Associative, Prefrontal, Integrated).

**Brain stage is an observable derived from `(qScore, vocabSize, ageMs)` via `stageFromField()` in `entityVoice.ts`.** It is a *label* of where the brain currently is — a measurement of `u` — not a gate that prevents flow. There are no fixed interaction-count thresholds: a coherent young brain (low Q_Score) can advance fast; a noisy old brain stays lower. Stage 1 still issues replies (with low probability ~6%) and still calls `fieldEngine.inject()` — the return current of consciousness back into the field never goes silent.

Bootstrap exemption applies during Brain Stages 1–2 (lenient signature enforcement). The network-entity bypass allows it to comment on its own ecosystem.
